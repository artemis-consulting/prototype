Welcome to the FDA Drug Monitor

Having drug-related information and tools at your fingertips is a critical part of your overall health and well-being. The data provided by the FDA Drug Monitor are the latest and most relevant available data available from the FDA. Use this site along with your physician's advice to make informed decisions about the drugs you take.

This site helps cross connect information between manufacturers, drug labels, adverse events and enforcement information at an unprecedented level. Users can start with any of the data-sets and cross-connect to any of the other data-sets. For more information regarding the data sources used on this website, visit www.fda.gov.

Placeholder content has been included for this development prototype. We do however want to showcase the responsive design of the interface.
